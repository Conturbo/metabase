DateTimeUnit

Valid unit for datetime bucketing.

Must be equal to one of these:
  • :quarter
  • :day
  • :hour
  • :week
  • :second
  • :default
  • :day-of-week
  • :hour-of-day
  • :month
  • :month-of-year
  • :day-of-month
  • :year
  • :day-of-year
  • :millisecond
  • :week-of-year
  • :quarter-of-year
  • :minute-of-hour
  • :minute
