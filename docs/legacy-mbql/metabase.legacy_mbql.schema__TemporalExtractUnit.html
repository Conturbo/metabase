TemporalExtractUnit

Valid units to extract from a temporal.

Must be equal to one of these:
  • :year-of-era
  • :quarter-of-year
  • :month-of-year
  • :week-of-year-iso
  • :week-of-year-us
  • :week-of-year-instance
  • :day-of-month
  • :day-of-week
  • :hour-of-day
  • :minute-of-hour
  • :second-of-minute
